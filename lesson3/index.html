Argentine Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Argentine Chamber of Commerce and Services (ACC) is an Argentine employers' organization created on November 7, 1924 under the name "Argentine Chamber of Culture, Commerce, Industry and Production". It is granted legal status on September 12, 1927, by order of the Executive Power, with its current name of Argentine Chamber of Commerce and Services.1 It is organized as a national confederal center of local and sectoral organizations of merchants, and companies dedicated to the trade. In 2016 the Argentine Chamber of Commerce changed its name to the Argentine Chamber of Commerce and Services.
